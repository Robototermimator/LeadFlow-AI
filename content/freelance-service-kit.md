# Freelance Service Version

## Service Description
LeadFlow AI Service helps businesses identify qualified local prospects and launch personalized email outreach campaigns quickly.

## Pricing Tiers
- Free Sample: 10 mock leads and one outreach sample
- Pro Service: 200 leads + 3-sequence email set ($299)
- Agency Service: 1000 leads + multi-segment messaging + onboarding call ($999)

## Deliverables
- Lead CSV
- Lead scorecard
- Outreach email sequence
- Follow-up playbook

## Client Questionnaire
1. What niche are you targeting?
2. What location(s) matter most?
3. What service/product are you selling?
4. What is your ideal customer profile?
5. Preferred outreach tone (formal, casual, consultative)?
6. Any competitors or examples you want to emulate?
